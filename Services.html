Venue Review

Riverfront Community Center

Address: 123 S Esplanade St, Leavenworth, KS 66048

Review: I went to this venue, the Riverfront Community Center, for a wedding this previous year. What I liked about this venue and what they offered to the
weddings was the beautiful rooms, the space, and the help that accompanied. There were two rooms for ceremonies across from one another, or if you decided to be
married on a day that church was held in session, it could be done. The sectionizing of the area still allowed ample privacy. There were more than enough chairs,
so that was not a problem to be faced. The area for the reception was spacious and just across a hall from the ceremony room. The Community Center staff offered
help with cleaning and setting up. There was a section of dance floor in this room that was tiled as apposed to carpeted.

Overall, I think this is a great place to hold any sort of event. While it was great for a wedding, the reception hall could be for any large party. The added scenery outside of the establishment
was nice, as well. The river is less than a hundred yards away, where there are river walks and statues. Great for photo opportunities.


9/10













Nicole Book
