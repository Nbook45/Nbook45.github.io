  Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

  About: This is the second movie in the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling.

  Review: This is both simultaneously a film critique as well as an entertainment critique, where Kansas City
   conducts live symphonies each year to one of the Harry Potter films. The symphony plays the music to Harry Potter, which there is
   quite a lot when you pay attention to it, and it is a beautiful experience. I would recommend this type of entertainment any day.
   The music is lovely and played by very skilled artists. This was at the Kaufman stadium, which is pleasant in itself.

   As for the movie, Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, it is about finding out who the heir of Slytherin is, who is petrifying mudbloods,
   and how will Harry Potter and his friends save the day. While it is not one of my most favorites of the series, it is important in the character
   building and strengthening of the friendship between the main characters. It plays on the skills of each character and how Harry always seems
   to find himself where he shouldn't be.


  9.5/10
