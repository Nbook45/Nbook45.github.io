  1. Mud bugs

  Address: 3524 NE Vivion Rd, Kansas City, MO 64119

  About: Mud bugs is a Louisianna Po' Boy style shop. They serve cajun couisine in the North Kansas City area.

  Eating: Blue crab cake po' boy.

  Review: I absolutely loved this place. They were quick with their orders, the atmosphere was enjoyable, and the smell was heavenly.
  I would definitely recommend this place to anyone who is in the mood for cajun, spicy, and filling food. Blue crab is one of my favorite seafoods,
  and they do a very spectacular job of fixing it up for you. The sauce that they slather on it is delicious, and everything compliments each component
  of this po' boy.


     9.5/10












Nicole Book
